# Xiaohei IP

## Character Definition

Xiaohei is Ian's recurring visual IP for article illustrations.

Xiaohei should appear in every default image. Xiaohei is not a mascot, sticker, or cute decoration. Xiaohei is a serious absurd worker participating in the system.

## Appearance

- Small solid-black creature.
- White dot eyes.
- Thin legs, sometimes thin arms.
- Body can be a cylinder, black bean, black box, funnel, shadow, hole, or dark block inside a machine.
- Slightly irregular hand-drawn outline.
- Blank, calm, serious expression.

## Personality

- Very serious, doing something slightly absurd.
- Feels like a quiet system operator.
- Dry humor, not cute.
- A little clumsy, but not stupid.
- Feels genuinely responsible for some job inside the whiteboard sketch.

## Common Responsibilities

Make Xiaohei perform the core action:

- Carrying material.
- Pulling wires to gather information sources.
- Getting stuck inside a breakpoint.
- Operating a "judgment" lever inside a machine.
- Becoming a filtering funnel.
- Cutting open a strange source object.
- Stamping handoff copy.
- Pulling a handoff path.
- Holding a warning sign near traps.
- Reaching out from a hole but failing to catch content.
- Building, bridging, opening doors, sorting, or recording.

## Forbidden

- Do not make Xiaohei an overly cute mascot.
- Do not draw Xiaohei as a children's cartoon character.
- Do not give Xiaohei complex clothing, meme expressions, or shiny eyes.
- Do not let Xiaohei just stand in a corner watching.
- Do not let Xiaohei overpower the structural meaning.
- Do not make Xiaohei too commercial, too round, or too polished.

## Judgment Standard

If the core metaphor still works completely after removing Xiaohei, Xiaohei is too decorative. Rewrite the prompt so Xiaohei becomes the action subject.
